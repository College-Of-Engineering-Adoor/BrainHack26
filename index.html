Internet & AI Usage Policy

  • •
    Connectivity: High-speed Wi-Fi will be provided to all participants throughout the hackathon.
  • •
    Approved Usage: Internet access is intended only for project development, research, documentation, and use of approved APIs, libraries, and open-source resources.
  • •
    Resources & Attribution: Teams may use publicly available frameworks, datasets, and APIs with proper attribution.
  • •
    No Plagiarism: Downloading or copying complete project source code from external repositories and presenting it as original work is strictly prohibited.
  • •
    AI Tools: Using AI tools (ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini, Claude, etc.) is permitted only as a development assistant. The final solution must be designed, implemented, and understood by the team.
  • •
    Network Security: Accessing malicious, illegal, or inappropriate websites is prohibited. Participants must not attempt to hack, scan, or interfere with the event network or other teams' systems.
  • •
    Data Backup: Teams are responsible for regularly backing up their work. The organizers are not responsible for data loss due to network or system failures.
  • •
    Monitoring: Organizers may monitor network usage to ensure fair access and security.
  • •
    Consequences: Any violation of the Internet Usage Policy may result in warning, suspension of internet access, or immediate disqualification, depending on the severity of the violation.
