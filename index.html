Common Queries

Who can participate?

Any tech enthusiast, developer, or designer currently enrolled in a college in Kerala.

What is the team size?

Teams can consist of 2 to 4 members. Solo participations are generally not recommended for a 12-hour sprint.

Is there a registration fee?

Registration details and potential fees are updated on the Google Form. Click Register to verify.
